Zici helps junior high students master Chinese vocabulary. Enter your email and password to log in. The home page shows every grade from 7 to 9, with mastery progress per unit. Open Grade 7, select a word, and tap AI Explain — DeepSeek returns pinyin, usage context, and an example sentence. The review page uses spaced repetition: flip the card, rate your recall, and the algorithm schedules the next session. The pinyin quiz tests pronunciation with multiple choice. Stats show mastery across every grade and semester.
